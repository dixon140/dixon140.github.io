Terminus Island

Beam Smasher

1. Activate Pack a Punch. As you activate the power stations, you must turn on three computers. The first is outside to the left of spawn. The second to the right of spawn. The third is at the bottom of sea tower.

2. Put Dead Wire on your gun with one of the Arsenal Machines around the map. You now must shoot electrical boxes in order around the map. As you are going up the lift, opposite the PAP machine, in the window. Then immediately to your right there will be another. Above the Pack a Punch machine at the top. In the bottom floor of living quarters. Outside of living quarters.

3. A door will open and you must kill the zombie trapped under the filing cabinet. It will drop a key fob, use it on the briefcase in sea stacks and pick up the item.

4. Make your way back to the secret room and interact with the computer. There will be a code you must enter which you can figure out here.

5. Once you have entered the code, proceed a round (or wait until round 9). And a blue orb will appear on the island displayed on the terminal in the room. Pick up the item from the machine and take it to the island. When you interact with the orb, it will occasionally shoot a zombie which you must kill and pick up the orb it drops and take it to the bigger orb. Once complete, the orb will move and you need to pick up the item again. Do this two more times at the bottom island, and the top left island.

6. You are now able to build the beam smasher on the table in the secret room.

Pack a Punch

1. Activate and defend the 3 highlighted generators around the map.

2. Dive into the water into the Bio Lab to activate the elevator and reveal Pack-a-Punch.
